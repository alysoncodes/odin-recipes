Zuppa Toscana

Ingredients

  • Broth
  • Ground sausage
  • Kale
  • Potatoes
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Saute the ground sausage until cooked through in a large soup pot.
  2. Add broth to the pot and begin to boil the sausage and broth mixture.
  3. Peel and dice potatoes and add them to the pot to cook (about 10 min)
  4. When the potatoes are just cooked, add the kale to wilt.
  5. Remove from the heat, and add the heavy cream.
  6. Chop the bacon and fry it in a pan.
  7. Serve the soup with bacon crumbles and parmesan cheese on top.
