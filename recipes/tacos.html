Chorizo Tacos

Ingredients

  • Chorizo
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Onions
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Green Sauce

Steps

  1. Pan fry chorizo until cooked.
  2. Prepare the toppings. Chop the cilantro, onion and lime.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a lightly oiled pan.
  4. Serve tacos in warmed tortiallas and topped with cilantro, onion and a sqeeze of lime.
