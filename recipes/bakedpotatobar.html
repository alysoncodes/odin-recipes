Baked Potato Bar

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Cheese
  • Onions
  • Chili
  • Sour Cream
  • Broccoli
  • Bacon
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Wash and wrap the potatoes in foil, then bake them in the oven for one hour.
  2. Prepare the toppings
  3. Serve all toppings in individual dishes and allow guests to customize their meal.
